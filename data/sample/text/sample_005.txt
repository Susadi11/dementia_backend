Sample Control Interview - Younger Control Participant

This is a transcript of an interview with a healthy younger control participant for comparison. Shows clear, coherent speech patterns typical of healthy cognition.

Interviewer: Hi there. How's your day going?
Participant: Great, thanks for asking. I'm excited to participate in this study.

Interviewer: Can you walk me through your typical work day?
Participant: Sure. I work in software development. I usually start my day checking emails and reviewing my team's progress. Then I have meetings to discuss project milestones and any blockers. After that, I focus on coding tasks. I usually take a lunch break around noon, and then continue with more development work or code reviews.

Interviewer: What do you enjoy most about your job?
Participant: I really enjoy the problem-solving aspect. Every coding challenge is like a puzzle, and I like figuring out elegant solutions. Plus, I enjoy collaborating with my team. We have good brainstorming sessions.

Interviewer: How do you handle stress at work?
Participant: I try to take breaks when I feel overwhelmed. I might go for a walk, grab a coffee, or step outside for some fresh air. I also exercise regularly, which helps manage stress. And I have a good support system with friends and family.

Interviewer: What are your future goals?
Participant: I'd like to move into a leadership role eventually. I'm also interested in learning new technologies and maybe specializing in AI or machine learning. Long term, I'd like to start my own company someday.

- Good attention to detail
- Confident responses
- Appropriate tone and pace

Sample Control Interview - Healthy Participant

This is a transcript of a typical interview with a healthy control participant. The person is coherent, answers questions directly, and shows clear logical flow in their speech.

Interviewer: Good morning. How are you today?
Participant: I'm doing well, thank you for asking. I'm glad to be here for this interview.

Interviewer: Can you tell me about your morning routine?
Participant: Sure. I wake up around six o'clock, have a cup of coffee, and then I usually read the newspaper or check the news online. After that, I get ready and have breakfast with my wife.

Interviewer: What do you typically have for breakfast?
Participant: Oh, usually some toast with jam or eggs and bacon. Sometimes oatmeal with fruit. My wife often makes pancakes on weekends.

Interviewer: That sounds nice. Do you exercise regularly?
Participant: Yes, I try to walk for about thirty minutes most days. I also do some light stretching and occasionally swim at the community center.

Interviewer: How long have you been doing this exercise routine?
Participant: I'd say for about three years now. I started because my doctor recommended it for my heart health, and I've noticed it really helps with my energy levels.

Note: This participant shows:
- Clear, coherent responses
- Logical flow of speech
- No repetition of questions
- Appropriate confidence levels
- Minimal hesitation
- Normal speech rate
